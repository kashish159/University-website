We Are The One Of The Largest Universities In The World

The value of service is central to the mission of Greenville as a liberal arts university. It infuses the passions and pursuits of our students, faculty, staff and alumni, and is essential to how Greenvillians serve the public good.The University has reinforced its commitment to helping students and alumni use their educations to not only benefit themselves but also society more broadly. We push students, faculty and alumni to think about how their research, education and lives will benefit the nation, the world and humanity, and give them the support and resources to make it happen.
